Recipe #1

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 12 slices
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 1/2 cups
Brown Sugar 1 cup
White sugar 1 cup
Baking Powder 2 teaspoons
Baking Soda 1 teaspoons
Cinammon 2 teaspoons
Ground Ginger 1/2 teaspoon
Nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon
Salt 1 teaspoon
Canola Oil 1/2 cup
Applesauce 1 cup
Almond milk 3/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tablespoon
Grated carrots 3 cups

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and prepare 2 round 8-inch cake pans by lining the bottom with parchment paper and spraying well with oil.

Step 2:In a large bowl, add all the dry ingredients and whisk well.

Step 3: Pour in the canola oil, applesauce, almond milk and vanilla and mix until combined with a large spoon. Now fold in the grated carrots. Be very careful not to over mix the batter, or your cake will come out dense and chewy!

Step 4: Pour the cake batter evenly between the prepared pans, then bake for 35-45 minutes, until a toothpick comes out clean and the tops of the cakes are set. Remove from the oven, and allow to cool for about 20 minutes before carefully removing from the pans.

Step 5: Once cooled completely, frost and then enjoy :)

Recipe #2

Ingredients Quantity
Water Full kettle
Coffee grounds 4 scoops
Almond, Oat, or Cocnut milk how ever much you want!
stevia or sugar how ever much you want!

Step 1: Boil your water and prepare your coffee grounds in the coffee pot.

Step 2: Once your water has finished boiling, pour your water into the coffee grounds and let the coffee drain into the pot.

Step 3: Pour your coffee in a mug and add youfr desired milk and sweetner! Enjoy! :)
